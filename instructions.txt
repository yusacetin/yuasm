loadimm rd val: load immediate
loaddir rd addr: load direct 

store addr rs: store direct

add rd rs1 rs2: add, store to rd
sub rd rs1 rs2: subtract, store to rd

lt rd rs1 rs2: less than
lte rd rs1 rs2: less than or equal to
gt rd rs1 rs2: greater than
gte rd rs1 rs2: greater than or equal to
eq rd rs1 rs2: equal to

jump val: unconditionally jump to section val (same as calling doing val())
jumpdir rs: unconditionally jump to val at rs

jumpif val rcond: jump to section val if cond>0
jumpifdir rs rcond: jump to val at rs if cond>0
